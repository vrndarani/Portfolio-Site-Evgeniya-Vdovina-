About

My Story

I am currently living in München, Germany. In my free time I read on psychology, fashion, social media marketing and explore this planet. As a former personal stylist, I love taking inspiration for my creativity from everywhere – nature colors I see outside, high-end designer silhouettes, or pieces of architecture. Fact about me: I love helping people and being around people, but I as an introvert I regularly need to escape to my cave and recharge to create more.

From 2019 to 2021 I have worked as personal stylist. I love identifying problems people have and providing solutions in a gentle and professional manner. I did SMM for my business, curated personalized digital style guides for people and eventually got carried away by everything design. As 2020 hit us with the urgent need for sustainability, and a lot went online for good, I decided to minimize my interaction with fast-fashion and bring use to the growing online reality: become a UX designer.
